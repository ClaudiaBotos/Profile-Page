Why me?

I am looking to improve all my skills by working harder and harder and prove myself to the world. The first step I took was signing up for Treehouse and starting to learn. Slowly I began to understand and to code. Coding is slowly becoming like an addiction, you want to know more, to do more. It is true than I am not very experienced yet, but this is about to change , as I will do my best.

What I plan to do to achieve my purpose?

I will not stop here and I will try to learn more, HTML and CSS are just the beginning. After I am done knowing all I need , I plan to learn Java Script and all the others lessons I need to become a front-end developer.
